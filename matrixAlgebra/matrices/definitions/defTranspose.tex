\begin{definition}
    The \emph{transpose} of an \(m \times n\) matrix \(A,\) denoted \(A^T,\) is the \(n \times m\) matrix whose \(i\)th row is the \(i\)th column.
\end{definition}

\begin{example}
    \[A = \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 2& 3 \\
        4 & 5 & 6
    \end{bmatrix}\]
    \[A^T = \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 4 \\
        2 & 5 \\
        3 & 6
    \end{bmatrix}\]
\end{example}

Using symbols, \(\left(A^{T}\right)_{i,j} = A_{j,i}.\)
