\begin{example}
    At the end of each month, \(\$175\) is deposited into a savings account that pays \(4\%\) compounding monthly. Find the balance in the account at the end of \(3\) years. What is the compound interest?
    \begin{solution}
        \(R = 175.\) There are \(12\) interest periods in a year so \(r = \frac{0.04}{12} = 0.0033\) and \(n = 3 \cdot 12 = 36.\)

        The future value of the annuity is \(175s\left(36,0.0033\right) \approx 6677.81.\)

        The compound interest is the difference between the amount of the annuity and the sum of the payments.

        \[6677.81 - 6300 = 377.81.\]
    \end{solution}
\end{example}
