\begin{definition}
    The formula
    \[S = P e^{rt}\]
    gives the compound amount \(S\) of a principal \(P\) after \(t\) years at an annual interest rate of \(r\) compounding continuously.
\end{definition}

Consider an account in which \(\$1000\) is invested at \(3.44\%\) compounding continuously compared to \(3.5\%\) compounding quarterly. The annually compounding amount is represented by the steps and the continuously compounding amount is represented by the curve.
